Pokepath TD

Pokepath TD is a strategic tower defense game that challenges you to think ahead, manage resources wisely, and adapt your tactics to survive increasingly difficult enemy waves. Every placement decision matters as enemies follow winding paths toward their destination.

Designed for both casual players and tower defense fans, the game delivers a balanced mix of accessible mechanics and deep strategic gameplay. As levels progress, new enemy patterns and tougher waves force you to refine your strategy and upgrade choices.

Gameplay Overview

In Pokepath TD, your goal is to stop enemies before they reach the end of the path. You do this by placing defensive units at key positions and upgrading them over time. Resources are limited, so choosing the right upgrades at the right moment is crucial.

Key Features

  • Strategic tower placement along predefined enemy paths
  • Progressive difficulty with increasingly complex enemy waves
  • Upgrade system that rewards smart planning and timing
  • Smooth performance optimized for both desktop and mobile browsers

Controls

  • Mouse / Tap: Place and upgrade units
  • Click: Select towers and interact with UI
  • Mobile: Fully playable with touch controls

If you enjoy tactical decision-making and classic tower defense mechanics, Pokepath TD offers a rewarding experience that keeps each level fresh and challenging.
